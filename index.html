Legal

Privacy Policy

LinkVault respects your privacy. We do not store your personal links on any server — all encryption happens in your browser. Anonymous analytics (visit count, country, device type) may be collected via third-party ad networks you connect.

What we collect

• Locally cached link history (in your browser only).
• Aggregate click counts via your ad provider.
• Standard server logs (IP, user-agent) on the static host.

What we do NOT do

• We do not sell your data.
• We do not require accounts to protect links.
• We do not inject malware or tracking pixels.
